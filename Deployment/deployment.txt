Deployment Phase

Deployment in SDLC is the process of releasing the completed software to a live environment where it becomes available for real users.

Two common deployment models are Manual Deployment and Continuous Deployment.

Common deployment tools include Jenkins, AWS, and Docker.

DevOps practices support deployment by automating processes, improving collaboration between development and operations teams, and enabling faster, more reliable software releases.

A real-world example is the Facebook outage in 2021, where a deployment-related configuration error caused global downtime affecting Facebook, Instagram, and WhatsApp.
