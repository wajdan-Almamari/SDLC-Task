Design Phase

The main deliverables of the Design Phase include system architecture diagrams, UI/UX designs, database design, and technical specifications.

Common tools used in the Design Phase include Figma, UML diagrams, and Lucidchart.

System design focuses on how the system works internally, including architecture and data flow, while UI/UX design focuses on how the system looks and how users interact with it.

A real-world example of poor design is the Healthcare.gov website, where weak system design and lack of scalability caused major failures during launch, increasing cost and failure risk.
